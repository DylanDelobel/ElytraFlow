# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-26

### Added
- Initial release.
- Automatic chestplate re-equip on landing — scans the full inventory and equips the best available chestplate when the player touches the ground while wearing an elytra.
- Items with Curse of Binding are always excluded from selection.
- Toggle keybind (unbound by default) under Options → Controls → ElytraFlow to enable or disable the mod at any time.
